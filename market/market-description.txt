Have your Infinitecoins always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Infinitecoin Wallet is the first mobile Infinitecoin app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Infinitecoin amount in BTC and mBTC.
• Conversion to and from legitimate currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Infinitecoin via NFC, QR-codes or Infinitecoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Infinitecoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for Infinitecoin balance.

You can find more about Infinitecoin at
http://infiniteco.in
